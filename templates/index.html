Soup or Dumpling?

The soup or dumpling theory posits that all food can be classified as a soup or a dumpling. Pizza, for example, is a deconstructed dumpling. Sometimes, however, it can be difficult to decide whether a particular food should be classified as a soup or a dumpling. The AI below uses machine learning to help you decide!
